Motivation to sign up
blockful aims to make DAO governance secure. We have developed a risk assessment framework, along with tools and a monitoring system, to better understand and contribute to the ecosystem, thereby reducing the risk of attacks and governance vulnerabilities in decentralized organizations.
By joining the Security Council, we bring both a governance-first perspective and hands-on technical experience. This dual expertise positions us to support the council in safeguarding Arbitrum’s infrastructure, DAO, and community.


Relevant experience
Recently, we helped secure the governance of Shutter, a DAO whose governance was vulnerable and could have lost $3M if Blockful had not identified the issues and proposed solutions to its members. Read the full case here: https://paragraph.com/@blockful/shutter-attack-prevention

Shutter is not the first DAO where we have identified vulnerabilities and proposed ways to protect its governance. It all started with ENS, where we uncovered a potential attack that could have drained its entire treasury, valued at over $150M. As a result, we contributed to the implementation of a Security Council at ENS to protect it from future threats: https://paragraph.com/@research.blockful/a-hidden-threat-to-ens-uncovering-and-solving-a-major-governance-risk

Building on this experience, we also assessed the security of Uniswap, where blockful received a grant to develop the Anticapture framework — our DAO security product — and implement it within Uniswap: https://uniswapfoundation.mirror.xyz/SAPBIdMcJpo\_gUUyHdMNuH8r7qpCqRtxFbDrui7Na-I

Beyond these cases, blockful has studied vulnerabilities across other DAOs in the ecosystem, aiming to deepen the understanding of governance and contribute to a more secure market. Compound is one such example, where we published two research pieces on attempted attacks against the organization:

* First attempt involved Humpy — a well-known DAO attacker — trying to pass a proposal to use Compound’s treasury for personal benefit: https://paragraph.com/@research.blockful/anatomy-and-antidote-for-compound-war
* Second attempt involved the Compound Foundation proposing a set of measures to protect the DAO’s governance, during which we identified Humpy moving addresses to potentially block the proposals: https://x.com/blockful\_io/status/2023530187221332393

None of the attacks on Compound were successful.

Additionally, risks have been assessed and analyses published on other DAOs such as Scroll, Nouns, and Gitcoin: [https://anticapture.com/](https://anticapture.com/)
